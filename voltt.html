VOLT: Verstile Online Lab Tool

For Real Ciruits and Electronics

The Idea

We want to make a small device people can work with to learn real circuits and electronics without needing experienced people present to teach.

This device will allow you to breadboard circuits and measure and power them directly. The power and measurements will be synced with an online service to follow online labs. The innovation of this project is in the online integration. People can physically do labs and validate everything they do online.

This is currently is demand by Engineering schools because teaching students circuits and electronics requires a lot of overhead - lab space, teaching assistants, homework and lab updates, and hardware. This device would save schools and students a lot of time and effort, as well as allow them to learn more effectively.
